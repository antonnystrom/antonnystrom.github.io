Gesture Level-Model

Background

I developed a model which can be used to quickly and easily evaluate the effectiveness of touch-based mobile interfaces, prior to creating prototypes. Over 1000 data points were collected from participants who partook in a observational experiment using a prototype specifically programmed for the study. This study was made for my master's thesis and it recieved the highest grade.

In 2019, the paper was cited in the International Journal on Advanced Science, Engineering and Information Technology (IJASEIT), which is an international peer-reviewed journal dedicated to interchange for the results of high quality research in all aspect of science, engineering and information technology.
